⚡ Quick Start — 4 steps
1
Import — tap + Import or the camera button to load your image
2
Size — set your engraving width and height in mm in the Presets tab
3
Material — pick your material. The app sets everything else automatically
4
Export — tap the red Export button at the bottom. Done

That is all you need. The Adjust and Dither tabs are optional fine-tuning.

📋 What the preset buttons do
⚡ SimpleStep-by-step guided wizard for beginners.
Auto PrepBalanced general-purpose preset. Good for most images.
Smart MDFAnalyses your image scientifically for MDF.
🪵 BasswoodTuned for pale basswood ply.
Perfect EngraveAuto-picks the best dithering algorithm for your image.
HQ ModeDoubles DPI for finer detail. Slower to process.
⚙ How the processing works
ResizeMitchell-Netravali bicubic resampling — same as professional print software.
GreyscaleRec.709 luminance (broadcast TV standard) — weights colours correctly for human perception.
TonalContrast, brightness, gamma applied in the correct scientific order.
DitheringConverts greyscale to pure black & white using error-diffusion or ordered patterns.
DPIExported PNG has DPI metadata embedded so your laser software knows the exact physical size.
⏱ Processing time

Most images process in 5–15 seconds. Large images at high DPI or with Floyd-Steinberg/Jarvis/Stucki dithering can take up to 3 minutes on mobile.

💡 Tips

Your settings are automatically saved and restored next time you open the app.

The A/B button in the preview lets you compare original vs processed by dragging the divider.
